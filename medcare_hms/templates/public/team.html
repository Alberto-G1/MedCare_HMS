THE WONAs is a team of passionate Computer Science and Engineering finalists dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare management through innovative technology solutions.

Our diverse skills and shared vision have come together to create MedCare HMS - a comprehensive hospital management system. With expertise spanning full-stack development, database design, user experience, and system architecture, we've built a platform that streamlines healthcare operations while maintaining the highest standards of security and usability.
